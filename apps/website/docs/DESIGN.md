# Design and motion system

## North star

**Follow the signal.** A single acid-lime signal begins as wind-tunnel flow, becomes a circuit, becomes telemetry, and ends as an inspectable local architecture. The page should feel like a calm endurance pit wall under pressure—not a collage of racing tropes.

## Visual system

- Near-black graphite world, bone evidence sections, one lime signal, and amber only for unresolved alpha boundaries.
- Compressed Archivo headlines behave like bodywork: wide, structural, and close to the frame edge.
- JetBrains Mono is reserved for provenance, state, measurements, and controls.
- Large surfaces stay quiet. Density is concentrated inside the product theatre where it has semantic value.
- Product screenshots are evidence. Generated imagery is atmosphere. The two are never presented as the same thing.

## Narrative

1. **Calibration** — the local signal and product promise establish the world.
2. **Circuit** — raw shared memory resolves into evidence.
3. **Pit wall** — four scroll beats demonstrate live state, analysis, strategy, and setup safety.
4. **Machine boundary** — the game, bridge, and Apex separate into local layers; the cloud route is explicitly absent.
5. **Proof** — test scope and truthful generated-data labels replace vague trust badges.
6. **Unknowns** — the motion quiets while validated and open boundaries are named.
7. **Green flag** — installer, portable build, checksum, and local demo complete the conversion path.

## Motion grammar

- Native scrolling only; no wheel capture or synthetic scroll.
- The page progress line maps to race sectors, not arbitrary percentage decoration.
- The hero uses one generated wind-tunnel image with restrained scroll parallax, aero flow, and a precise pointer reticle.
- Product mode changes use a 180–220 ms eased entrance. Scroll pose changes are reversible.
- Magnetic CTA travel is limited to a few pixels and only runs on fine pointers.
- Architecture tilt is CSS 3D and retargetable from its current transform.
- One-time marketing reveals can be longer; frequent controls remain below 300 ms.
- Reduced motion removes parallax, pinning, and transform entrances while retaining state/color feedback.

## Interaction states to preserve

- Header: transparent top, legible scrolled material, full keyboard focus.
- Mobile menu: modal, inert background, Escape close, no behind-the-sheet scroll.
- Product tabs: scroll-selected on desktop; directly selectable everywhere; no essential auto-advance.
- Telemetry: pointer scrub plus focus and Arrow Left/Right.
- Strategy: safe/balanced/attack each update laps, reserve, risk, and stint geometry.
- Setup: every row updates the one-change preview and always keeps backup/rollback visible.
- FAQ: native details behavior with one open answer at a time.

## Performance budget

- Immediate route: HTML/CSS/React, self-hosted fonts, and the AVIF poster.
- No WebGL dependency or 3D payload; the cinematic hero stays under 60 KiB in AVIF.
- Cap DPR, disable shadows, pause offscreen, and dispose geometry/materials on teardown.
- Keep reference videos and generation sources out of the public and committed build.
